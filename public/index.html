Singapore Regulatory Framework

What Applies to Your Singapore Business

Key binding regulations under Singapore's sector-based compliance approach. Most SMEs are affected by 3–5 of these.

RegulationApplies ToDeadlineStatus
SGX Sustainability Reporting All SGX-listed companies 4 months after FY end Binding
Climate Reporting (Non-listed) Revenue ≥S$1B + Assets ≥S$500M From FY2027 Upcoming
Carbon Tax (CPA) Industrial facilities ≥25,000 tCO2e/year S$45/tonne from 2026 Binding
Mandatory Energy Management (ECA) Companies using ≥54 TJ/year Ongoing annual Binding
Water Efficiency (WEMP) Premises using ≥60,000 m³/year Annual PUB submission Binding
Mandatory Packaging Reporting ≥S$10M turnover + ≥50 tonnes packaging Annual NEA submission Binding

EU Regulatory Framework

What Applies to Your EU-Facing Business

The EU's compliance-by-design framework — stricter, broader scope, with substantial financial penalties for non-compliance.

RegulationThreshold / ScopeKey DeadlineStatus
CSRD — Corporate Sustainability Reporting >1,000 employees + >€450M turnover FY2027 data (report 2028) Binding
CBAM — Carbon Border Adjustment Importers of steel, cement, aluminium, fertilizers, electricity, hydrogen ACD by 31 Mar 2026 Active Now
EmpCo — Greenwashing Prohibition All companies marketing to EU consumers Enforcement Sept 27, 2026 Upcoming
CSDDD — Due Diligence Directive >5,000 employees + >€1.5B turnover Phase 1: July 2029 Upcoming
ESPR — Ecodesign (unsold goods ban) Textiles, footwear, electronics producers July 19, 2026 Upcoming
Pay Transparency Directive All employers in EU member states National laws from June 7, 2026 Upcoming
